About

A builder who cares about every step of the funnel.

I started in customer support a decade ago and grew into Shopify development by solving the gaps I saw every day — slow product pages, leaky checkouts, email flows that never sent. That dual lens is still how I work.

Today I lead end-to-end Shopify builds: UX architecture, custom Liquid theme development, conversion-rate optimization, Klaviyo email infrastructure, B2B and affiliate setup, technical SEO, and 3PL coordination. I sweat the details on performance and Core Web Vitals because they show up in revenue.

When the build is live, I stay close to the data — A/B tests, search rankings, support tickets — and keep iterating. The goal is always the same: a fast, conversion-focused storefront with operations that don't break behind the scenes.

How I work

From brief to fully shipped.

A four-stage engagement that takes a brand from its first conversation through launch and into the ongoing optimization that compounds over years.

01

Discovery

Week 1 — 2

Audit the current storefront (or starting point), map brand goals to a build plan, and surface the wins hiding inside what's already there. You leave this stage with a scoped roadmap, not a sales pitch.

  • Brand & positioning review
  • Tech audit + Core Web Vitals baseline
  • UX gap analysis
  • Build roadmap & timeline
02

Build

Week 3 — 8

Custom Liquid theme work, performance tuning, content and catalog migration, and app integration. Built in staged previews so you can see the storefront come together — no big-reveal surprises at launch.

  • Custom Shopify theme in Liquid + JS
  • Catalog & content migration
  • App integration & customization
  • Performance & accessibility passes
03

Launch & CRO

Week 8 — 12

Go-live, then immediately into measurement. A/B tests on the highest-traffic templates, Klaviyo flows firing, technical SEO foundation laid down. Launch is the start of the work, not the end of it.

  • Production launch & QA
  • Klaviyo flows: welcome, abandoned cart, post-purchase
  • A/B testing on PDP, cart, checkout
  • Technical SEO & schema baseline
04

Ongoing partnership

Month 4 onward

The phase that defines whether a Shopify store actually grows. Continuous optimization, support coverage, growth experiments, and 3PL coordination — the quiet compounding work my long-term partners stay for.

  • Continuous CRO & design iteration
  • Email program & campaign management
  • SEO, B2B & affiliate growth
  • 3PL ops & CX support
